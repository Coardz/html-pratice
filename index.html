Which Roblox game had the most visits in 2022?

Roblox is one of the most popular video games worldwide. It boasts over 50,000,000 users daily, and the previous year significantly impacted Roblox’s growth. In this piece, we answer one of many questions that players have about the platform: which Roblox game had the most visits in 2022? Even though this data is not officially tracked, the most visited Roblox game is extremely requested information.

WHAT IS THE MOST VISITED ROBLOX GAME IN 2022?

According to official information, Adopt Me is the most visited Roblox game of all time. At the moment of writing, the game has 31.9 billion visits. Moreover, the game significantly increases in popularity year by year. It is nearly impossible to find a Roblox fan who has not played it. Therefore, most players consider Adopt Me the most visited Roblox game in 2022, but it's not true according to the statistics. The most visited Roblox game of 2022 is Brookhaven, which took second place in the list of all-time most popular games. At the end of 2021, the game was visited 12.2 billion times. Nowadays, Brookhaven has an insane 26.4 billion visits, meaning that the game was visited more than 10 billion times in 2022. Surprisingly, Brookhaven and Adopt Me have no competitors among other Roblox games. They are regularly updated, and they continue to increase in popularity even in 2023. It seems that their top spots for the most visited Roblox game will last for many years.

WHAT IS ROBLOX BROOKHAVEN?

Most Roblox fans know about Brookhaven, but some players still have no clue about this game. Roblox Brookhaven is a unique RPG created by Wolfpaq and Aidanleewolf. It was created in April 2020 and has gotten over 20 billion visits since. Brookhaven is a large city where you can become rich and buy giant houses and luxury cars. Plenty of vehicles and buildings in this game are pretty accessible, which is one of the main reasons for Brookhaven’s popularity. However, such simplicity is not always a good thing. Continue reading the guide, and you will find out three main reasons why players criticise Brookhaven.

WHY DO PLAYERS CRITICISE BROOKHAVEN?

The main reason why many Roblox players refuse to play Brookhaven is that the game has transformed into a dating platform for children as opposed to its initial role-playing mechanics. The second reason Roblox players criticise Brookhaven is that it has developed a toxic community. And the last reason is that the game has now become significantly monetised. Battle Passes impact your gameplay and can even increase your car speed, and if you refuse to contribute real money to the game, you might just be constantly called a noob.
